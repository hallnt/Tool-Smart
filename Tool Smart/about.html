About Us

About 5 years ago, Patrick and Colleen Hall shipped a small barrell of used power tools. When they unloaded the barrel, there were only about 10 pieces of tools. Colleen placed them on a table at the front of her gate and passersby began to stop and check them out. They used the money to buy more tools and stocked the unused building in their front yard, and this was the birth of Tool Smart.

Tool Smart is located in Bay Road, Little London, Westmoreland, Jamaica. We sell a variety of new and used name brand power tools, from Jackhammers to small drills, table saws, chain saws, generators and more. Our tool brands include:

  • Dewalt
  • Bosch
  • Milwaukee
  • Porter Cable
  • Husky
and more. We offer competitive prices and customers can call and reserve a tool. There's a tool for everyone.
